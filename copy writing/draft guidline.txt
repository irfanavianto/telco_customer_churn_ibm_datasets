-----------------
Most churn prediction projects focus on maximizing accuracy. In reality, churn modeling is about deciding who deserves attention first.

Unfortunately, evaluation metrics alone rarely answer the real business question — which customers should we act on, and why?

This project approaches churn prediction from a business-first perspective. Using telco customer data, the objective is not to build the most accurate model, but to translate predictions into practical retention decisions that can be executed by the business.

Rather than optimizing metrics in isolation, this analysis focuses on how churn models support decision-making: identifying high-risk customers, understanding the drivers behind churn, and prioritizing actions that balance impact and cost.
